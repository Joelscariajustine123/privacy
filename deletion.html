How to Request Account Deletion

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data from our systems. Please follow the simple step below to initiate this process.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When your account deletion is processed, the following actions will be taken:

  • Your user account and authentication credentials (email) will be permanently removed from Firebase Authentication.
  • Your entire private data collection in Firestore, including all courses, faculties, and student records you have created, will be permanently deleted.
  • This action is irreversible. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

Initiate Deletion Request

To start the process, please click the button below to send us an email request. No additional information is needed in the email.

Request Account Deletion via Email

Processing Time

Please allow up to 7-14 business days for your deletion request to be fully processed. We will send a confirmation email to your registered address once the deletion is complete.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team.
